# Experimental Log

> Pre-writing data tables and observations for the ketamine/opioid sex-dependence study.

---

## Experimental Design

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Species | Sprague-Dawley rats |
| Imaging modality | Functional ultrasound imaging (fUSI) |
| Ketamine dose | 10 mg/kg IV (fUSI) or IP (behavior) |
| Naltrexone dose | 10 mg/kg SC |
| NTX pretreatment timing | 10 min before ketamine |
| Imaging planes | Bregma +2.5 mm, -3.5 mm |
| Within-subject design (fUSI) | 3 sessions: VEH+KET, NTX+KET, NTX+VEH |

## Experiment 3: Gonadectomy Reversal

| Group | Opioid-Dependent CBV Change | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------------------|---------------|
| Intact males | Present (NTX suppresses ketamine effect) | Opioid-dependent |
| Gonadectomized males | Absent (NTX does not suppress) | Requires male gonadal hormones |
| Intact females | Absent | No opioid dependence |

Fig. 3: Gonadectomy fully reversed the opioid-mediated effects in males.

---

## Experiment 4: MK-801 Control (Selective NMDAR Antagonist)

| Drug | Opioid Dependence of Neural Response | Interpretation |
|------|--------------------------------------|---------------|
| Ketamine | Yes (in males) | Not purely NMDAR-mediated |
| MK-801 | No (NTX does not suppress) | NMDAR-selective effects not opioid-dependent |

This dissociation demonstrates that the opioid-dependent component is specific to ketamine and not a general feature of NMDAR antagonism.

| Test | Context | Key Results |
