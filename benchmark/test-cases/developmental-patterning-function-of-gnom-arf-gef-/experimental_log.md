# Experimental Log: GNOM ARF-GEF Plasma Membrane Function

## GN vs. GNL1 localization

| Protein | PM localization | Long-lived PM structures | Clathrin-coated pits | Golgi localization |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| GN | Yes | Yes (distinct from CCP) | No colocalization | Yes (WT) / No (GNfewerroots) |
| GNL1 | Weak/absent | No | No | Yes |

## gn knockout phenotype analysis

| Process | gn knockout effect | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Clathrin-mediated endocytosis (CME) | Normal | GN dispensable for CME |
| Secretion | Normal | GN dispensable for secretion |
| Developmental patterning | Severely disrupted | GN essential for patterning |
| Root polarity | Abnormal | GN required |

## GNfewerroots mutant characterization

| Property | GNfewerroots | Wild-type GN |
|---|---|---|
| Golgi localization | Absent | Present |
| PM localization | Present | Present |
| Developmental patterning | Partially functional | Fully functional |
| Root phenotype | Fewer roots | Normal |

## Brefeldin A (BFA) treatment

| Condition | GN behavior | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-BFA | PM + Golgi | Normal localization |
| Post-BFA | Redistribution from Golgi | PM pool persists; Golgi pool disrupted |
| BFA washout | Golgi recovery | Dynamic cycling |

## Notes

- 2024-01-08: Live imaging confirms GN at PM in structures distinct from clathrin pits.
- 2024-01-25: gn knockout lines show normal CME and secretion -- major surprise.
- 2024-02-10: GNfewerroots mutant confirms PM as primary site of developmental action.
- 2024-02-28: BFA experiments support revised model: GN developmental role is PM-based.
- Key insight: current textbook model of GN functioning at the Golgi needs revision.
