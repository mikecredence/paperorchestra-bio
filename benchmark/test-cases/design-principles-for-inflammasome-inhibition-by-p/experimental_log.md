# Experimental Log: Inflammasome Inhibition by POPs

## POP1 target specificity

| Target | POP1 inhibition | Previous report | Our finding |
|---|---|---|---|
| ASC (adaptor) | Poor inhibitor | Reported as strong inhibitor | Contradicted |
| AIM2 PYD filament | Inhibits assembly | Not tested | New finding |
| IFI16 PYD filament | Inhibits assembly | Not tested | New finding |
| NLRP3 PYD filament | Inhibits assembly | Not tested | New finding |
| NLRP6 PYD filament | Inhibits assembly | Not tested | New finding |

## POP2 target specificity

| Target | POP2 activity | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| ASC nucleation | Direct suppression | Blocks initial nucleation |
| AIM2 filament elongation | Inhibits | Caps growing filament |
| NLRP6 filament elongation | Inhibits | Caps growing filament |

## Comparison of POP1 vs. POP2 mechanisms

| Feature | POP1 | POP2 |
|---|---|---|
| Primary target | Upstream receptor PYD filaments | ASC nucleation + receptor filaments |
| ASC inhibition | Poor | Strong (direct) |
| Receptor filament inhibition | Assembly blockade | Elongation blockade |
| Receptors affected | AIM2, IFI16, NLRP3, NLRP6 | AIM2, NLRP6 |

## Methods used

| Approach | Application | Key result |
|---|---|---|
| Rosetta in silico | Predict POP-PYD binding interfaces | Identified differential binding modes |
| In vitro filament assay | Measure filament assembly inhibition | Quantified POP1/POP2 inhibitory effects |
| In cellulo inflammasome assay | Functional validation in cells | Confirmed selective inhibition profiles |
