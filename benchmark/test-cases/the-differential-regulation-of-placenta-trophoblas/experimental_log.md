# Experimental Log -- Placental BPGM in FGR

## 2024-01-15 -- Murine hypoxia model characterization

Set up acute and chronic gestational hypoxia cohorts. Controls kept at normoxia (21% O2). Chronic hypoxia at ~13% O2 for defined gestational windows. Acute hypoxia short-term exposure protocol also running.

### Fetal weight outcomes

| Group | n | Mean fetal weight (g) | % reduction vs control | FGR incidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Normoxic control | 12 | 1.25 | -- | baseline |
| Acute hypoxia | 10 | 1.08 | -13.6% | elevated |
| Chronic hypoxia | 11 | 0.95 | -24.0% | significantly elevated |

Both acute and chronic hypoxia recapitulate FGR phenotype. Chronic is more severe as expected.

## 2024-02-10 -- Placental morphometry (MR imaging + histology)

| Parameter | Control | Acute Hypoxia | Chronic Hypoxia | Direction |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Labyrinth area (mm^2) | 4.8 | 3.9 | 3.2 | Decreased |
| RBC density in labyrinth (cells/field) | 28 | 45 | 62 | Increased |
| Spiral artery diameter (um) | 85 | 110 | 135 | Expanded |
| Placental weight (mg) | 102 | 95 | 88 | Decreased |

Key finding: hypoxia shrinks labyrinth zone and dilates spiral arteries. More trapped RBCs in labyrinth under hypoxia.

## 2024-03-05 -- BPGM and 2,3-BPG analysis

### IHC scoring for BPGM (murine placentae)

| Compartment | Control (H-score) | Chronic Hypoxia (H-score) | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trophoblast | 145 | 85 | <0.01 |
| Labyrinth | 120 | 72 | <0.01 |
| Decidua | 90 | 88 | ns |

BPGM markedly reduced in trophoblast and labyrinth under chronic hypoxia. Decidua unaffected.

### Metabolomics -- 2,3-BPG concentration

| Sample type | Control (uM) | Chronic Hypoxia (uM) | Fold change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Murine placenta | 38.5 | 22.1 | 0.57x |
| Murine maternal blood | 42.0 | 50.3 | 1.20x |

Placental 2,3-BPG drops while maternal blood 2,3-BPG trends up -- consistent with compensatory response.

## 2024-04-20 -- Human FGR validation

### IHC in human placentae

| Marker | Control (n=15) H-score | FGR (n=18) H-score | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|
| BPGM | 160 | 92 | <0.001 |
| Hemoglobin | 110 | 155 | <0.01 |

Human FGR placentae mirror the murine findings: lower BPGM, higher hemoglobin staining. Validates the translational relevance of the mouse model.

## Summary of key numbers

- Chronic hypoxia reduces labyrinth area by ~33%
- Spiral artery diameter increases ~59% under chronic hypoxia
- BPGM H-score in trophoblast drops ~41% with chronic hypoxia
- Placental 2,3-BPG decreases to 0.57x of control levels
